## II. The Joseph Pattern

In the account of Joseph in Genesis:

* Years of abundance were followed by years of famine
* Insight came before crisis
* Preparation happened before pressure

Joseph’s response was structured:

* He gathered one-fifth during the years of plenty
* He stored resources in an organized way
* He created a system—not just a reserve

When famine came, that preparation:

* Sustained Egypt
* Supported surrounding nations
* Preserved future stability
